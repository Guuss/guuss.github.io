Rogue_Spart3n

Lore Writer since 2015

"Everyone has something that truly activate that nerve in their system, or something that answers the question, what to do in my down time. For me, lore is one of those things. I first embraced the adoration of the worlds and the nearly endless constraints that fantasy and imagination can have when I was young. Over time, that passion developed, and I worked, and still work, with my friend through the years on creating our own fantasy novel. Writing is a passion, and while for me it may not be a career, it is something that feeds into my desire to build worlds and develop plots, people, and their stories through my own words. Everyone has an art, lore is mine, but the question still remains, is it yours too?"
